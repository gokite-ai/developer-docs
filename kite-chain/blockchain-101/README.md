# Blockchain 101

If you are new to blockchain development, this section will cover the foundational tools and concepts to help you set up your environment and deploy your first smart contract on the Kite AI Testnet. This section will provide fundamental knowledge about blockchain technology and concepts that are essential for understanding and developing on the Kite platform.

## What You'll Learn

- **Blockchain Fundamentals**: Understanding the core concepts of blockchain technology
- **Cryptography Basics**: How cryptographic principles secure blockchain networks
- **Consensus Mechanisms**: Different ways blockchains achieve agreement
- **Smart Contracts**: How programmable logic works on blockchains
- **DeFi Concepts**: Understanding decentralized finance principles
- **Web3 Development**: Introduction to building decentralized applications

## Getting Started

If you're new to blockchain technology, we recommend starting with the fundamentals and working your way through each topic. Each section builds upon the previous one to give you a comprehensive understanding of blockchain development.
